Visit Laguna Beach

Laguna Beach is a coastal city located in Southern California. It is known for its beautiful beaches, art galleries, and outdoor activities such as hiking and surfing. The city has a population of around 23,000 people and covers an area of approximately 9 square miles. Laguna Beach is a popular tourist destination, particularly in the summer months, and attracts visitors from all over the world.
