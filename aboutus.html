Bountiful Foods

A Little More About us

Bountiful Foods is a small, family-owned store located in Carlsbad, California, that specializes in fruit drinks and fruit bags. The store was founded by a local family in the 1980s, who saw a need in the community for a store that offered healthy, fresh fruit options. They started out by selling their fruit drinks and fruit bags at local farmers' markets, but soon opened a small storefront in the heart of Carlsbad. Over the years, Bountiful Foods has become a beloved fixture in the community, known for its commitment to using only the freshest, highest quality fruits in their products. Despite facing competition from larger chain stores in the area, Bountiful Foods has remained dedicated to their mission of providing customers with healthy, delicious options that support local farmers and promote a healthy lifestyle.
